Corona vs Misinformation: A pandemic within a pandemic?

When news of the COVID-19 broke out, panic took root as it would with a novel virus outbreak. Even in the digital age, “alternative facts” proved to be the pandemic within the pandemic. Africa seemed to be hit hard by the phenomenon, as civic knowledge was temporarily left to unreliable sources. Was the pandemic partly fueled by misinformation? One could have a strong case suggest so.

Science vs Politics: The global warming dilemma

Shockingly, 5 of the warmest have all occurred since 2015. However, when climate change is brought up, it quickly takes a political deviation. Clear scientific facts are politicized throwing the situation to even graver jeopardy. There is wavering consensus from leaders as the climate changes for the worst. Should there be a strictly autonomous body to regulate climate law as a whole? Many would be for it.
